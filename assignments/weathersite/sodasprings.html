Winter Weather Safety Class

by David Johnson

The Lion's Club hosted their annual winter saftey class yesterday at the community center. It was well attended and, as usual, community response was overwhelming. Topics covered included; how to jump a battery, frostbite prevention, recognition and treatment, and saftey kits for your car. If you missed the class all of the information may be found on the Lion's club website or at the community center. Stay warm!
